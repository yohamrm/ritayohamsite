Suzanne Collins Website Redesign

Project Overview

The objective for this project was to redesign author Suzanne Collins' website. Her website that she currently has is not user friendly so we were tasked with redesigning it in order to make it usable.

The Process

First, I identified my target audience for Collins' website and their motivations. I also analyzed the website for any improvements that need to be made. I then settled on five design decisions that I would tackle throughout this redesign project. One of my goals for this redesign included changing the product page to categorized columns and rows, rather than one column of a long list of her work. Another goal was to create a footer that includes the quick links that are already present on the site. I also changed the brand to better match the aesthetic of her popular series, The Hunger Games, and also fixed the landing page to allow users to better understand what website they are on. Finally, my final goal was to allow users to easily purchase her novels on her website. This is a feature that is not currently available on her website, but I felt would be very important to have on her website. After establishing my goals, I implemented them into high fidelity mockups. Lastly, I created my final redesign through HTML and CSS. You can view all the work I did for this project below.
