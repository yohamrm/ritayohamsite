The Breeze

Job Overview

I serve as the Creative Director of Advertising at James Madison University's student run newspaper, The Breeze. My responsibilities include setting up the layout for the pages each week so the editors can add in their content and completing the ad layout. I also manage two ad designers on my team and assist with designing ads both for The Breeze and for those advertising in our print paper or on our website. I also update our media book each year which is what we distribute to potential advertisers each year. Additionally, I interview and hire new personnel in the advertising department and oversee their training when they onboard. I communicate with the editor in chief in order to ensure the advertisement layout works well with their content and plans for the paper that week.
