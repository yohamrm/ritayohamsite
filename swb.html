Switchboard Internship

Job Overview

From June 2021 through August 2021, I completed a Development Associate Internship at a start up company called Switchboard which is located in Washington D.C. Switchboard is a product that allows businesses to send out opt in SMS, MMS, and video texting to keep in contact with their customers and send information through text. I had a variety of projects that I worked on, but my main role was to help improved the usability of their product and assist with designing new features the company wished to implement. I completed a presentation along with another intern on ways that I believed their product could be improved upon. I worked closely with the CTO in order to start the process of implementing these changes. Some features I got to design include a feature that allows users to transfer money, a chat bot, and a plan calculator. This internship allowed me to use the skills that I had been developing in my classes and use them on a product in the business world. The experience I gained from this internship was vital to my understanding of UX and interface design in the real world.
