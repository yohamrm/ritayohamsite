Overview

As Public Relations Chair, my role was to assist with the creation of promotional materials in order to gain more members, organize and create posts for our Instagram, and design merchandise. I created all of these designs through Adobe Illustrator. These opportunies allowed me to use the skills I have been learning through my classes for a projects on campus.
