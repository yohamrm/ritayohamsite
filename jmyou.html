JMYou App Prototype

Project Overview

The objective for this project was to design an application by making a high fidelity prototype in Adobe XD and base that on user research that you conduct with your team members. My group decided to create an app for JMU students that allows them to access all the different interfaces that the university uses in one central hub. We conducted extensive research that ultimately led us to designing a high fidelity prototype of what we believed the application should look like.

The Process

We started our project by identifying a problem that needed to be solved. James Madison University uses various different websites and applications that students have to keep up with and use for their everyday life as a student. They attempt to have a central hub for this through a product called "MyMadison," however this product is not user friendly and does not have a mobile option for students. Therefore, we decided so solve this problem through our application. We conducted user research through interviews with JMU students, identifying related work, and finding user needs for the app and compiled them into various reports. We then brainstormed design solutions, developed a low fidelity prototype, and did more user testing now using our prototype. Finally, we developed our high fidelity prototype, conducted even more user testing, and noted any other changes that needed to be added to our product in the future. We compiled all this information into a final proposal which you can view below.
